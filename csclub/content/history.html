CS Club History:

The BSU Computer Science Club was founded in the spring semester of the 2007-2008 academic year by a handful of CS students who sought to enhance the collegiate experience of all Computer Science students and to spread their enthusiasm for the discipline. The following is a record of activities, speakers, and noteworthy events that have taken place in the history of the BSU CS Club.
