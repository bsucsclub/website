Win a Laptop for Attending Meetings and Events!

About:

The generous people at Federated Insurance have donated to the CS Club a laptop, and we have decided to give it away to one lucky member. For every meeting and event a member attends they will be able to put their name in for a drawing. We will be having a club party next April to elect officers and to draw for the laptop. The more times you attend the better your chances are to win! The club would like to thank Becky Dawley and everyone from Federated for this awesome gift!
